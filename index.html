Patrick Larabee

sysadmin, developer, tech enthusiast

I have a passion for technology and learning how things work. I am primarily interested in the web, linux, and automation. I currently work as a Systems Administrator for a local non-profit organization. In my current role, I maintain Windows server infrastructure that is key to the organization and to the well-being of its' clients.

Prior to this, I worked for the University of Central Florida supporting College of Sciences research faculty. I was member of a team that provided desktop engineering solutions some of the many colleges within U.C.F. It was here that I was introduced to automation through configuration management and scripting. It also sparked a fascination with linux and programming.

In the time since, I have been on a journey to learn anything and everything I can about computing, software development, and related technologies. Outside of work I regularly complete online courses, work with my homelab, and develop personal programming projects such as web applications.

Outside of technology, I can be found reading or spending time with my wife and two australian shepherds in Orlando, FL.
